HELLO!

I am a freelance illustrator with the good fortune of splitting my time between San Francisco and Manila. I took the scenic route in my career, starting in the Northern California redwoods as an environmental educator, through the Yale School of Forestry, and on to work as an landscape designer. My work was about making the invisible processes of nature visible.

These days, I use ink and paper to visualize the emotional core of stories and concepts, often through the use of figures and natural forms. I can also render plans and perspectives for landscape architecture and engineering.
